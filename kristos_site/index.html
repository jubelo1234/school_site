About Us

Kristos Ark School offers high-quality and affordable educational services to prepare children for a successful future. Our goal is to support every child, regardless of their background, to be morally and spiritually sound, mentally alert, skillful, fit, healthy, safe, and responsible members of society. Our school takes pride in serving the community and stands out due to its fully equipped and age-appropriate facilities, secure environment, and closed-circuit camera system(CCTV) for remote parent viewing. Additionally, Our Resource Center supports staff development and research efforts.

Curriculum

Educational Philosophy

Our educational philosophy centers on providing a strong foundation for our students. We begin with the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), offering a smooth transition to full-time schooling through a part-time induction period. This period fosters familiarity, individual assessment, and a nurturing environment.

The EYFS curriculum encompasses seven areas of learning, spanning physical development, communication, literacy, and more. As students progress to Key Stages 1 and 2, we blend the Nigerian and British Curricula to offer a well-rounded education, preparing them for academic success and global citizenship.
